Brand Overview

Coca-Cola is a global leader in the nonalcoholic beverage industry, offering an extensive portfolio of approximately 200 brands across more than 200 countries and territories. Since serving its first glass in Atlanta in 1886, the company has evolved into an iconic powerhouse, now delivering 2.2 billion drinks each day, including sparkling soft drinks, water, sports drinks, coffee, tea, juice, dairy, and plant-based beverages. Coca-Cola owns 30 billion-dollar brands such as Coca-Cola, Sprite, Fanta, Minute Maid, and fairlife, and leverages a robust global bottling and distribution network to ensure its products are accessible and relevant to a diverse and ever-changing consumer base. Building on its heritage of innovation and trusted quality, Coca-Cola maintains a strong market position by consistently expanding its portfolio and adapting to local tastes. The company has demonstrated resilience and effective cost management amid economic and currency challenges, achieving steady volume and organic revenue growth, especially in emerging markets. With a stable financial foundation, ongoing investment in product development, and a commitment to sustainability and social impact, Coca-Cola continues to set the standard for the beverage industry worldwide.

Competitive Advantages

  • Unmatched global distribution and footprint (sold in 200+ countries)
