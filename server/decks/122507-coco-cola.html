Brand Overview

Coca-Cola is a global leader in the non-alcoholic beverage industry, offering an extensive portfolio of approximately 200 brands across more than 200 countries and territories. Since its founding in 1886, the company has evolved from serving a handful of daily drinks to delivering 2.2 billion beverages every day, encompassing sparkling soft drinks, water, sports drinks, coffee, tea, juice, and value-added dairy and plant-based options. Key brands such as Coca-Cola, Diet Coke, Fanta, Sprite, and fairlife underscore its market dominance and ability to capture diverse consumer preferences worldwide. Renowned for its robust bottling and distribution network, Coca-Cola consistently drives growth through product innovation, localized marketing strategies, and operational excellence. Despite economic headwinds and currency pressures, the company continues to strengthen its value share in the global non-alcoholic ready-to-drink market, propelled by strong performances in emerging markets and successful launches of new products. With a resilient balance sheet and a clear focus on sustainability, cost management, and innovation, Coca-Cola remains well-positioned to navigate market complexities and deliver long-term value to consumers and shareholders alike.
